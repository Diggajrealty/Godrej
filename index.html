Location Advantages

Bannerghatta Road is one of South Bangalore's most prominent real estate hubs, transforming rapidly with the addition of the Yellow and upcoming Pink Line Namma Metro. Living at Godrej Vanantara means you are never far from the city's pulse, yet nestled in a serene 36-acre forest reserve.

The location provides unparalleled access to major IT corridors including Electronic City, ORR, and the Central Business District via the NICE Road. The micro-market is fully mature, offering world-class infrastructure right at your doorstep.

About Begur Hobli

Begur Hobli, situated just off Bannerghatta Main Road, represents the perfect balance of heritage and modernity. Known for its historical significance, the area is now a bustling residential haven for tech professionals and families alike.

Residents enjoy proximity to prestigious educational institutions like Greenwood High, top-tier healthcare facilities like Fortis and Apollo, and massive retail destinations including Royal Meenakshi Mall. The nearby Bannerghatta National Park ensures the air quality remains superior to the rest of the city.

Frequently Asked Questions About Godrej Vanantara Bannerghatta

What is Godrej Vanantara on Bannerghatta Road? +

Godrej Vanantara is a 36-acre forest-themed luxury township by Godrej Properties located on Bannerghatta Road, South Bangalore. It features 2008 premium apartments across 18 high-rise towers (G+31/32 floors) in 2 BHK, 3 BHK, and 4.5 BHK configurations, blending lush greenery with high-rise sophistication.

What is the price of apartments in Godrej Vanantara Bannerghatta Road? +

Prices start at ₹1.57 Crore for a 2 BHK apartment (1250 sqft). 3 BHK-2T apartments start at ₹2.08 Crore (1650 sqft), 3 BHK-3T at ₹2.52 Crore (2000 sqft), and 4.5 BHK at ₹3.65 Crore (2900 sqft). All prices are inclusive of base cost and may be subject to additional charges.

What is the RERA number of Godrej Vanantara? +

The RERA registration number of Godrej Vanantara is PRM/KA/RERA/1251/310/PR/130526/008653. BDA Approval: BDA/NA.MA/S/A.A-3/TA-2/S/04/2026-27.

Where exactly is Godrej Vanantara located? +

Godrej Vanantara is located at Next to Greenwood High School, off Bannerghatta Main Road, Begur Hobli, Bengaluru South - 560083, Karnataka, India.

What is the possession date of Godrej Vanantara? +

The expected possession date of Godrej Vanantara is December 31, 2031. Construction commencement date is May 15, 2026.

What BHK configurations are available at Godrej Vanantara? +

Godrej Vanantara offers 2 BHK (1250 sqft), 3 BHK-2T (1650 sqft), 3 BHK-3T (2000 sqft), and 4.5 BHK (2900 sqft) apartments ranging from 1250 to 2900 sqft across 2008 units in 18 high-rise towers on a 36-acre campus.

What is the price of Godrej Vanantara in Bangalore? +

The price of Godrej Vanantara in Bangalore starts at ₹1.57 Crore onwards for a 2 BHK luxury apartment. The 3 BHK units are priced between ₹2.08 Cr and ₹2.52 Cr, while the expansive 4.5 BHK premium units start at ₹3.65 Crore.

Is Godrej Vanantara RERA approved? +

Yes, Godrej Vanantara is fully RERA approved by the Karnataka Real Estate Regulatory Authority. The official RERA registration number is PRM/KA/RERA/1251/310/PR/130526/008653. It also holds BDA approval.

What are the possession dates for the project? +

The possession date for Godrej Vanantara is slated for December 31, 2031. Construction is scheduled to commence in May 2026, adhering to strict quality and timeline standards set by Godrej Properties.

Which BHK sizes are available? +
