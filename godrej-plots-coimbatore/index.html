FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Godrej Plots Coimbatore — FAQs

Where exactly is Godrej Plots Coimbatore located? +

Godrej Plots Coimbatore is located in Chettipalayam, South Coimbatore, directly opposite the iconic 150-acre Coimbatore Golf Club. The project enjoys excellent road connectivity to Avinashi Road, Race Course Road, RS Puram, Peelamedu, and the Coimbatore International Airport.

What are the plot sizes available in Godrej Plots Coimbatore? +

Plot sizes at Godrej Plots Coimbatore range from 800 sq.ft. to 3,000 sq.ft. The most popular bracket is 1,200–1,800 sq.ft. — ideal for a duplex or G+2 villa with ample garden space. Golf Club-facing plots in the 1,800–3,000 sq.ft. category are the most premium and have limited availability.

What is the price of plots in Godrej Plots Coimbatore? +

Prices start from approximately ₹50 Lakhs* for compact plots (800–1,200 sq.ft.). Standard plots (1,200–1,800 sq.ft.) start around ₹70 Lakhs*, and premium Golf Club-facing plots (1,800–3,000 sq.ft.) start from ₹1.10 Cr*. All prices are indicative and subject to change. Contact our team for the current official price list.

Is Godrej Plots Coimbatore RERA approved? +

Yes. Godrej Plots Coimbatore is DTCP, LPA, and RERA approved. We strongly recommend verifying the RERA registration number directly on the official Tamil Nadu RERA portal (tnrera.in) before making any financial commitment to ensure current compliance status.

What amenities are included in the Godrej Plots Coimbatore community? +

The gated community includes a grand clubhouse, swimming pool, gymnasium, spa, cricket pitch, cycling track, jogging trail, landscaped gardens, children's play area, senior citizen deck, pet park, 24/7 CCTV gated security, 24/7 water supply, power backup, underground utilities, and wide internal roads. All infrastructure is designed to the Godrej Properties standard.

Is investing in a plot in Coimbatore a good decision in 2025–2026? +

Absolutely. Coimbatore is among India's top 5 fastest-growing Tier-2 real estate markets. The upcoming Coimbatore Metro, strong IT and industrial employment base (L&T IT Park, Tidel Park), growing organised gated community demand, and now Godrej Properties' market entry — all point to strong, sustained price appreciation. Plotted developments in South Coimbatore near the Golf Club represent a rare combination of location premium + brand premium + infrastructure upside.
