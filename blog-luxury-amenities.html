Introduction

When you invest in Godrej Vanantara on Bannerghatta Road, you are not just buying an apartment; you are securing a lifestyle deeply integrated with nature. With over 80% open spaces, this 36-acre township boasts some of the most luxurious and thoughtfully designed amenities in South Bangalore.

The 70,000 Sq.Ft. Clubhouse

At the heart of Godrej Vanantara lies a massive 70,000 sq.ft. clubhouse, acting as the epicenter of community life. Equipped with a state-of-the-art gymnasium, private mini-theater, indoor badminton courts, and exclusive co-working lounges, it offers a perfect balance of recreation and productivity.

Forest-Themed Infinity Pool

Nothing says luxury quite like a sprawling infinity pool overlooking an urban forest. The pool area includes dedicated kid's wading zones, a sunken bar, and temperature-controlled jacuzzis, providing a resort-like experience right outside your door.
